About Us

We are more than just a coffee shop — we are a community of coffee lovers who believe in quality, comfort, and connection.

Our Story

Cozy Bean started as a small neighborhood spot in 2020. Our dream was simple: bring people together over great coffee and homemade pastries. From day one, our mission has been to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

Our Team

Friendly, passionate, and trained baristas who put love into every cup.

Our Coffee

Sustainably sourced beans roasted to perfection for the best flavor experience.

Our Values

  • Quality: We serve only the best beans and ingredients.
  • Community: A place where everyone feels welcome.
  • Sustainability: We care about our planet and reduce waste whenever possible.
